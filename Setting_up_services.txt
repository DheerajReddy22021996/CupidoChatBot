Setting up conversation and discovery:-
	Create a conversation instance on IBM cloud.
	You will need a free trial account on IBM.
	Go to the search bar and search for "conversation" or "discovery" service
	In the results choose the service and plan accordingly and create it.
	After creating,go to the dasboard and click on the instance you just created.
	After that launch your service or take a note of credentials.
	For Conversation,you can import the provided json to create intents,entities and dialog flow.
	For discovery news you just record the parameters like username,password,collection and environment id.
